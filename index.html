Bus Routes

Parents/guardians: The bus routes for the 2021-2022 school year are attached below. For ease of viewing we have listed the routes in the following categories:

Your child resides in Goffstown or Pinardville and is attending Goffstown High School, the route will be listed as GHS AM or PM

Your child resides in Goffstown or Pinardville and is attending Mountain View Middle School, Maple Avenue School, Bartlett or Glen Lake School, the route will be listed as GOF ELEM-MV AM or PM

Your child resides in Pinardville and is attending Bartlett or Glen Lake or Mountain View Middle School, the route will be listed as BART ELEM AM or BART ELEM-MV-GL PM

Your child resides in New Boston and is attending Goffstown High School, the route will be listed as a NB HS-AM or NB HS-MV PM.

Your child is attending NBCS or MVMS the AM route will be listed as NB ELEM -MV.

Your child attends NBCS the PM route will be listed as NB ELEM PM

***New Boston MVMS students travel with the high school students in the PM. their route is listed under NB HS-MV PM***

Due to bus driver shortages, bus routes have been consolidated and the actual route numbers may be different than in past years.

Both the Goffstown Truck Center (STA) and the School District acknowledge that should there be continued driver shortages, further bus route consolidations may occur.
